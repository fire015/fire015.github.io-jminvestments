LONDON COLNEY, ST ALBANS

Newly Refurbished Air Conditioned Offices to Let on Flexible Terms

A magnificent Grade 2 listed building comprising of just 13 modern office suites located moments from Junction 22 of the M25 and the Colney Fields Shopping Park which includes Sainsbury's, Marks and Spencer, Boots, Next and many other household names.

Berkeley House is easily reached from the M25 and M1 and offers convenient access by public transport - St Albans city station is in close proximity. Luton Airport is a 20 minute drive and Heathrow and Stanstead Airports are 40 minutes away.

The building has been tastefully renovated to provide flexible office suites from 72 sq ft up to 600 sq ft.

Some office suites are fully air conditioned and parking facilities are also available

Facilities include Air Conditioning, Broadband, Cat 5 /6 cabling, Wi Fi etc.
